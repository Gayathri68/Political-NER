Political news analysis in India is complicated by the sheer linguistic diversity of the country. Standard NER tools trained on English or single-language corpora fail to capture entities in regional-language political coverage. This project addresses that gap by providing a multilingual NER model specifically tuned to Indian political entities, enabling better information extraction from news articles, social media, and government documents across languages.
